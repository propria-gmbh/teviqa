Core Technologies

TEVIQA's application-specific solutions deliver unmatched performance in their respective domains while sharing common technological foundations.

Industrial

TEVIQA PSC

Proactive Surface Care

High-velocity oxy-fuel (HVOF) systems designed for on-site industrial protection in energy, oil & gas, and processing industries. Mobile deployment enables proactive maintenance exactly where needed.

Field-deployable units for in-situ repairs
50-70% reduction in equipment downtime
Patented particle delivery system
Aerospace

TEVIQA HPF

Hypersonic Particle Flow

Next-generation aerospace coatings with ultra-dense microstructure capable of withstanding extreme temperatures and velocities, ideal for rockets, satellites, and high-performance aircraft.

Thermal stability up to 1,800°C
Microstructural density >99.5%
Validated for re-entry conditions
Medical

TEVIQA IHC

Intellectual Hydroxyapatite Coating

Smart, bioactive coatings for dental and orthopedic implants with superior osseointegration and potential for embedded sensor integration, enabling the next generation of intelligent implants.

Bio-compatible ceramic matrix
30-40% faster osseointegration
Sensor-ready platform technology
